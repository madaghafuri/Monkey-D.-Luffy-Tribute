Monkey D. Luffy

The man who will become the pirate king.

Here is what he has done in times of his career as a pirate:

  • Alabasta - Defeated one of evil Shicibukai, Crocodile and saving the whole kingdom from organized coup d'etat
  • Skypiea - Defeated the proclaimed god at Skypiea and ending the 400 years war that was happening in Skypiea
  • Enies Lobby - Saving his kidnapped crew, Nico Robin, who trying to save his crew from World Government and escaping the Buster Call
  • Thriller Bark - Defeated one of the Shicibukai, Gecko Moria, and saving a lot of people who got their shadow stolen by Moria.
  • Marineford - He wrecked havoc at one of the biggest war in One Piece history, along with the other prisoners who he freed from Impel Down. Unfortunately he failed to save his brother, Ace, and Ace died at spot.
  • Fishman Island - Saving the Ryugu Kingdom and defeating Hordy Jones, the person who attempted a coup d'etat on Ryugu Kingdom and bring hopes to unity and peace between human and fishman/mermaid.
